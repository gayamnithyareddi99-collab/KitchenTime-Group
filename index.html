Our Services

Six Signature Hospitality Services

KitchenTime Group offers a comprehensive suite of hospitality and bar management services each precision-crafted to transform your venue into a thriving, guest-loved destination.

Service A

Full Bar Management

End-to-end daily bar operations creating a vibrant atmosphere for hotel guests and local community alike.

We oversee every aspect of bar operations: opening and closing procedures, key-holder responsibilities, stock replenishment, equipment maintenance, and compliance with health and safety regulations. Our managers coordinate seamlessly with kitchen staff and F&B managers to minimise service delays and maximise guest satisfaction across every service period.

Service B

Cocktail List Development

Bespoke signature cocktail menus crafted to increase revenue and guest loyalty.

Our mixologists design custom cocktail menus featuring original signature drinks, seasonal specials, and expertly curated spirit, wine, and beer selections. Every menu is built around your brand identity and target guest profile engineered to increase upsell opportunities, raise average spend, and create the "wow" moments guests remember and return for.

Service C

Event Hosting & Promotions

Organised special events and themed promotions that attract new guests and energise your venue.

From cocktail masterclasses and themed evenings to corporate hospitality and private hire management, our events team plan and execute memorable experiences that fill seats and generate buzz. We handle logistics, bar staffing, menu pairing, and on-the-night coordination so you can focus on welcoming your guests while we handle the details.

Service D

Inventory & Stock Control

Precise daily stock management and supplier coordination to eliminate waste and protect margins.

Our team implements rigorous daily stock-taking routines, supplier ordering schedules, and wastage reduction protocols ensuring your bar is always fully stocked, costs are controlled, and profit margins are protected. We maintain drought beer systems, manage cellar stock, and perform regular equipment cleaning and maintenance to ensure optimal functionality and compliance.

Service E

Guest Experience & Loyalty

Personalised, attentive service that transforms first-time visitors into loyal regulars.

Our bartenders greet every guest warmly, remember names and drink preferences, and deliver consistently attentive service from arrival to departure. We implement loyalty-building techniques personalised recommendations, birthday recognition, regulars' programmes that increase repeat visits and positive word-of-mouth referrals, making your venue the destination guests choose again and again.

Service F

Compliance & Responsible Service

Full alcohol law compliance and responsible service protocols to protect your licence and guests.

We implement comprehensive responsible alcohol service protocols including guest intoxication monitoring, Challenge 25 enforcement, and licence condition compliance protecting your venue's licence and reputation. Our team is fully trained in UK alcohol licensing legislation, food hygiene standards, and health and safety regulations, ensuring your venue operates legally, safely, and professionally at all times.

FAQ

Common Questions

What types of venues does KitchenTime Group work with?

+
KitchenTime Group works with a wide range of hospitality venues across London including independent restaurants, boutique hotels, private members clubs, corporate event spaces, and multi-site hospitality groups. Whether you operate a 20-cover neighbourhood bistro or a 300-seat hotel restaurant, our services are tailored to your specific venue, guest profile, and revenue goals. We have direct experience across Central London's vibrant hospitality scene, having worked with establishments in Greenwich, the West End, and City of London locations.

How does KitchenTime Group increase bar revenue for my venue?

+
We use a combination of proven revenue optimisation techniques: expertly designed cocktail menus that encourage premium selections, trained upselling techniques for sides, desserts, and drinks upgrades, and strategic event programming that fills seats on quieter weeknights. Our bartenders are trained to build rapport with guests remembering preferences and making personalised recommendations that feel natural rather than pushy. Venues managed by KitchenTime Group typically see a 25–35% increase in average bar spend within the first three months of our engagement.

Are your bartenders trained in responsible alcohol service and UK licensing law?

+
Absolutely. All KitchenTime Group staff are fully trained in UK alcohol licensing legislation, responsible service protocols, and Challenge 25 age verification. We monitor guest intoxication levels throughout every service period and follow strict protocols for discontinuing service when necessary protecting both your guests and your venue licence. Our team is also fully trained in health and safety regulations, food hygiene standards, and bar cleanliness requirements, ensuring your venue consistently meets or exceeds compliance standards.

Can KitchenTime Group design a completely new cocktail menu for our venue?

+
Yes cocktail list development is one of our signature services. We begin with a consultation to understand your brand identity, guest profile, and existing beverage offering. Our team then designs a bespoke menu featuring original signature cocktails, seasonal specials, and carefully curated wine, beer, and spirits selections. Every drink is costed, priced, and presented in a way that maximises your margin while delighting guests. We also create non-alcoholic alternatives and pair recommendations, ensuring every guest has a memorable experience at the bar.

What does full bar management actually include on a day-to-day basis?

+
Our full bar management service covers every aspect of daily bar operations. This includes opening and closing the venue (with key-holder responsibilities), setting up the bar ready for service, managing guest orders, preparing drinks across all categories, coordinating with kitchen and front-of-house teams, maintaining daily stock levels and placing replenishment orders, operating and maintaining bar equipment, hosting special events, and ensuring compliance with all health, safety, and licensing requirements. We also provide monthly performance reports covering beverage costs, revenue per cover, and guest feedback summaries.
